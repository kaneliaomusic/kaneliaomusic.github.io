Creation of the World (Genesis 1:1-31), what's on kaneliao's mind that could serve others?

In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth (Genesis 1:1). On the first day, He created light and separated it from darkness (Genesis 1:3-4). On subsequent days, God formed the sky, land, seas, plants, animals, and finally humans in His image (Genesis 1:26-27). On the seventh day, He rested, declaring His creation "very good" (Genesis 2:1-3).
